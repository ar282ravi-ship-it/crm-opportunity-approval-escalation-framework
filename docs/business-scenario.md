# Business Scenario

## Scenario

A sales organization manages enterprise opportunities in Dynamics 365 CRM.

Certain opportunities require managerial approval before progressing due to:

- High revenue value
- Excessive discount percentage

Managers often delay responding to approval requests, resulting in:

- Sales delays
- Revenue bottlenecks
- Manual follow-ups
- Poor approval visibility

---

## Proposed Solution

An automated Power Automate approval escalation framework was introduced.

The solution:

1. Detects high-value opportunities
2. Sends approval request to manager
3. Sends reminders every 24 hours
4. Escalates to senior manager after repeated delays
5. Updates approval status inside CRM

---

## Benefits

### Faster Decision Making
Reduces approval delays.

### Reduced Manual Effort
Eliminates repeated manual follow-ups.

### Better Governance
Ensures accountability through escalation.

### Improved Visibility
Approval status is tracked directly inside Dynamics 365 CRM.
